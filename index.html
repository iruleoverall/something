This is the official home of the Great Horned Owl. The Great Horned Owl, also commonly reffered to as the tiger owl or the hoot owl is a large owl native to the Americas. The owl is also the most widely distributed owl within all America

General Information

  1. Habitat

    The Great Horned Owl is found all acroos America, up to the green tree line. They are typically spotted in secondary-growth woodlands, swamps, orchards, and agricultural areas, but are also able to be viewed from mixed foerests.

  1. Diet

    Great Horned Owls are said to have the most diverse diet of all North American raptors. Their prey ranges from tiny rodents, to hares and geese. They primarily eat mammals and birds, but they also eat other species such as moles, chipmunks, sqiurrels, and bats.
